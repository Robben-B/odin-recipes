chicken tacos

Tender and spicy chicken tacos, cooked in adobo, chipotle peppers and topped with lemon. Serve with toppings of choice.

ingredients

  • boneless/deboned chicken thighs
  • chipotle peppers
  • adobo
  • garlic
  • salt
  • cumin
  • paprika
  • smoked paprika
  • lemon
  • chili powder
  • olive oil
  • corn tortillas

steps for tortillas

  1. place corn tortilla(s) into hot pan over medium high heat
  2. once heated to desired temp, remove tortilla and set aside
  3. repeat steps 1-2 until you reach to desired amount of heated tortillas

steps for chicken

  1. place chicken thighs inside a bowl
  2. lightly coat the chicken with olive oil
  3. season the chicken generously with chili powder, cumin, paprika, and smoked paprika
  4. let the marinate sit for at least 20 minutes (if the marinate is left for more than 20 minutes ensure it is put in a refrigerated area)
  5. thinly dice fresh garlic
  6. pour a thin layer of olive oil in a hot pan over medium high heat
  7. place chicken skin side down in the hot pan
  8. season chicken generously with salt
  9. leave chicken on medium high heat for approximately 6 minutes each side or until evenly cooked
  10. poor abodo and chipotle peppers over cooked chicken
  11. turn down to medium low heat
  12. shred chicken in the adobo (if shredding in pan, ensure the pan is non-stick and avoid using metal utensils to avoid scrapping off non-stick coating into food)
  13. squeeze 1 lemon over shredded chicken and use the juice to deglaze the pan
  14. chicken is ready to serve
