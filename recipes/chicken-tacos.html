chicken tacos

Tender and spicy chicken tacos, cooked in adobo, chipotle peppers and topped with lemon.

ingredients

  • boneless/deboned chicken thighs
  • chipotle peppers
  • adobo
  • garlic
  • salt
  • cumin
  • paprika
  • smoked paprika
  • lemon
  • chili powder
  • olive oil

steps

  1. place chicken thighs inside a bowl
  2. lightly coat with olive oil
  3. season generously with chili powder, cumin, paprika, and smoked paprika
  4. let sit for at least 20 minutes
  5. chop fresh garlic
  6. pour a tablespoon of olive oil in a hot pan
  7. place chicken skin side down in the hot pan
  8. season chicken with salt
  9. leave chicken on heat for ~6min each side or until evenly cooked
  10. poor abodo over cooked chicken
  11. shred chicken in the adobo
  12. squeeze 1 lemon over shredded chicken
  13. serve!
