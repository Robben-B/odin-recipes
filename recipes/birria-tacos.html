birra tacos

delicious birra tacos served with consomé. Goes great with cilantro

ingredients

  • chuck roast
  • onion
  • garlic
  • dried whole chili peppers
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cilantro
  • cumin
  • oregano
  • chili powder
  • cinnamon
  • beef bouillon
  • corn tortillas

steps for birria

  1. pour approximately 8 cups of water into a large pot over high heat
  2. chop chuck roast into large chunks and place into pot
  3. chop onion into quarters and place into pot
  4. peel whole garlic and place into pot
  5. place 6-8 whole dried chili peppers into pot
  6. place 1-2 cubes of beef bouillon into pot
  7. season generously with salt, pepper, cumin, oregano, chili powder, and cinnamon
  8. reduce heat to a simmer and let sit for 3-4 hours
  9. after 3-4 hours take out the garlic, onion, and dried chilis and place into a blender
  10. add approximately 1 cup of broth to the blender
  11. blend it together until it is a smooth liquid
  12. poor the blend back into the pot
  13. mix and let simmer until everything is emulsified
  14. take the chuck roast out of the pot and place into a large bowl
  15. shred the chuck roast
  16. take a corn tortilla and dredge it through the broth (consomé) and place it into a hot pan over medium high heat
  17. place some shredded chuck roast onto the tortilla
  18. fold the tortilla and ensure both sides are cooked
  19. serve with a side of consomé and top with cilantro
