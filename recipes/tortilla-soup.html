tortilla soup

delicious spicy chicken soup served with a side of tortillas to dip

ingredient

  • chicken breast
  • onion
  • garlic
  • chicken broth
  • can diced/crushed tomatoes
  • can corn
  • chili powder
  • cumin
  • smoked paprika
  • paprika
  • adobo seasoning
  • jalapeno
  • corn tortilla
  • olive oil
  • lemon
  • lime
  • vegetable oil

steps for tortillas

  1. pour vegetable oil into a pan over medium heat
  2. evenly slice tortilla into strips
  3. ensure oil is hot and place sliced tortillas into oil
  4. fry the tortilla strips until golden
  5. once cooked remove torillas from oil and place aside
  6. season the fried tortillas with salt
  7. repeat steps 1-6 until you reach to desired amount of fried tortillas

steps for tortila soup

  1. place chicken breast into large bowl
  2. thinly coat chicken with olive oil
  3. season generously with chili powder, cumin, paprika, smoked paprik and adobo seasoning
  4. let the marinate sit for at least 20 minutes (if the marinate is left for more than 20 minutes ensure it is put in a refrigerated area)
  5. finely dice, garlic, jalapeno and onion then set aside
  6. lightly coat the bottom of a large pot with olive oil over medium high heat
  7. place chicken into the hot pot
  8. season chicken generously with salt
  9. leave chicken on medium high heat for approximately 6 minutes each side or until evenly cooked
  10. once cooked take chicken out of the pot and set aside
  11. lightly coat the bottom of the pot with olive oil
  12. place your diced garlic, jalapeno and onion into the hot pot
  13. once cooked/aromatic pour in the can corn, can tomatoes, and chicken broth
  14. chop the cooked chicken breast and place into pot
  15. generously season the soup base with chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, paprika, salt and adobo seasoning
  16. squeeze 1 lemon into soup
  17. squeeze 2 limes into soup
  18. turn heat to high and let the soup reduce
  19. once reduced, taste and adjust seasonings to your preference
  20. soup is ready to serve!
